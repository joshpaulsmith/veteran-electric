Utility Division

Veteran Electric’s Utility Division supports critical electrical infrastructure projects across Northern Ontario with dependable crews, disciplined execution, and a strong safety-first work culture. From field support and maintenance work to metering and line-related infrastructure projects, our team is built to contribute where reliability, coordination, and workmanship matter most.

Built for Demanding Utility Environments

Utility work requires more than electrical knowledge alone. It demands planning, communication, site awareness, and crews that understand the importance of safety, scheduling, and consistency. Veteran Electric brings a workforce shaped by union training, field discipline, and the work ethic that comes from military veteran leadership.

We approach utility-related work with a practical mindset: show up prepared, work safely, coordinate effectively, and complete the job to a professional standard. Whether supporting contractors, utilities, or project partners, our focus is always on being a dependable part of the team on site.

Utility Infrastructure Services

Substation Maintenance

Planned and ongoing substation maintenance support, inspection assistance, equipment servicing, and site-based electrical work completed with close attention to reliability, documentation, and safety procedures.

Energy Metering

Metering installation, maintenance, and support services performed with a focus on accuracy, system performance, and dependable field execution.

Power Line Construction Support

Support for power line construction and related utility infrastructure work, including field coordination, crew support, and electrical project execution in demanding conditions.
