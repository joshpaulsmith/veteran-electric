Utility Division

Veteran Electric’s Utility Division supports critical electrical infrastructure projects across Northern Ontario. Our team brings discipline, safety, and reliability to every project, with a workforce built on strong union training and the work ethic of military veterans.

Utility Infrastructure Services

Substation Maintenance

Planned and ongoing substation maintenance, equipment support, and infrastructure service work completed with a strong focus on safety, documentation, and reliability.

Energy Metering

Metering installation, maintenance, and support services delivered with attention to accuracy, reporting, and system performance requirements.

Power Line Construction

Support for power line construction and related electrical utility projects, with a team that understands field conditions, safety standards, and project coordination.
