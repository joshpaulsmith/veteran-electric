Careers at Veteran Electric

Build a Career With a Team That Takes Pride in the Work

Veteran Electric is a unionized electrical contractor based in Sault Ste. Marie, serving clients across Ontario with dependable electrical and utility services. We are building a workforce around discipline, professionalism, safety, and pride in doing the job right.

Whether you are an experienced electrician, an apprentice, a field-ready worker, or a Veteran transitioning into civilian industry, we are looking for people who want to grow with a company that values strong standards and dependable execution.

Why Join Veteran Electric

Unionized electrical contractor with long-term opportunity
Proudly Veteran-supportive and team-oriented culture
Opportunities in both electrical and utility work
Professional, safety-first job environments
Based in Sault Ste. Marie and active across Ontario
A company focused on workmanship, reliability, and growth

Who We’re Looking For

Electricians & Apprentices

We are looking for people who care about quality work, accountability, and safe job execution. If you take pride in technical skill and dependable performance, we want to hear from you.

Utility & Field Workforce

Our Utility Division continues to create opportunities for disciplined, dependable individuals who are comfortable in field-based environments and infrastructure-related work.

Veterans Transitioning to Civilian Careers

We value the leadership, structure, work ethic, and professionalism veterans bring to a team. If you are looking to build a long-term civilian career, Veteran Electric is a strong place to do it.

What We Value

We value people who show up prepared, work safely, communicate clearly, and take ownership of the job. Our goal is to build a team that clients trust and employees are proud to be part of.

Growth With Purpose

As demand grows across residential, commercial, industrial, and utility-related work, we are creating real opportunities for the right people to build careers with strong standards, strong leadership, and long-term potential.
