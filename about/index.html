About Veteran Electric

Veteran Electric Corp is a proudly Veteran- and Aboriginal-owned local business dedicated to serving Sault Ste. Marie and communities across Ontario with dependable residential, commercial, and electrical service solutions.

Built on Strong Values

Veteran Electric Corp is a proudly Veteran- and Aboriginal-owned local business dedicated to serving the community of Sault Ste. Marie and beyond. Built on strong values of integrity, hard work, and respect, we have grown from a local operation into a trusted electrical service provider across Ontario.

We specialize in both residential and commercial electrical services, delivering reliable solutions tailored to each project. Whether it’s a small repair, a full installation, or an upgrade using modern smart home technology, our team is committed to getting the job done right the first time.
