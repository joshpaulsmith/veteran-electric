About Veteran Electric

Veteran Electric is a unionized electrical contractor based in Sault Ste. Marie, built on professionalism, workmanship, and a strong commitment to dependable service. We proudly employ military veterans and continue to grow to meet demand across residential, commercial, industrial, and utility-related work.
